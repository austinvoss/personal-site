About Me

Hello, my name is Voss. I'm a passionate full-stack software engineer with a love for problem-solving and creating innovative solutions. I am currently honing my skills in a full-stack software engineer bootcamp, and I'm excited to apply what I've learned in real-world projects.

Background

My journey into software development began when I started working with a loved one to apply an autoimmune protocol (diet) to address their health challenges. Soon after, I helped a friend with a similar diet following complications from surgery. Recognizing the potential to make a positive impact, I taught myself HTML, CSS, Ruby, and Ruby on Rails to build an app that made planning meals on the protocol easier.

The app utilized a user-friendly interface and incorporated real-world or practical measurements for foods, such as "1/2 avocado" or "1 fillet of salmon" instead of grams, making it simpler for users to plan their meals. After the success of this app, I started a business to share it with the world. I initially worked with clients directly through phone consultations, but later automated the process to guide clients through a 21-day diet program.

The app was used by thousands of people, leading to improvements in heart health, weight loss, reduced epileptic seizures, and heightened presence in autistic individuals. The income from this app allowed me to travel throughout Southeast Asia for a year, further fueling my passion for creating meaningful solutions through technology.
